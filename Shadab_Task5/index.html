About

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was one of India's greatest scientists and the 11th President of India. He played a major role in India's missile and space programs and inspired millions of young people through his speeches, books, and simple lifestyle.

Major Achievements

🚀 ISRO Scientist

Played a key role in developing India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III).

🛰 Missile Program

Led India's Integrated Guided Missile Development Programme.

🇮🇳 President

Served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

📚 Inspiration

Inspired millions through books like Wings of Fire and his motivational speeches.

Inspirational Quote

"Dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep."
